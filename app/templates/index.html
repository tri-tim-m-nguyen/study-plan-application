Welcome to the Study Plan Tool

This is a tool to help you create and manage your study plans.

About

A helpful website for students to help plan out their schedule...

This website will help you:

  • Help analyse how much time you spend on each assessment
  • Visually plan out your schedules
  • Allow you to compare your schedules with your friends
  • Help you find free time in your busy life
  • Provide insight on how much time you should put into each activity
  • Help you keep ontop of all your assessments

Purpose

The purpose of this application is to help students manage their study time effectively and efficiently ...

  • Manage their study time effectively and efficiently
  • Organize tasks and assignments based on priority
  • Visualize workload and deadlines clearly
  • Reduce stress by planning ahead

How to Start

Click to learn how to begin using the Study Plan Tool...

Getting started is easy! Follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Signup Page and create your account.
  2. Once signed in, navigate to the "Create Timetable" page.
  3. Fill in the spots on your timetable with different activities you plan to do.
  4. Save and create your new timetable.
  5. After creating timetables, go to the "View Timetable" page.
  6. From there, you can:
    • Edit your existing timetables.
    • Send compare requests to your friends to compare timetables.
  7. When your friend accepts the compare request, they can choose which of their timetables they want to compare with yours!
